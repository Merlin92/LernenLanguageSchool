About Lernen



Lernen is a vibrant, friendly school and we have a great mix of students from all over the world. We also know that our language programmes are only as good as the team we have working here at Lernen. That is why we do all we can to ensure we have in place experienced and enthusiastic staff who enjoy working with language students. We offer opportunities in a range of areas – teaching, administration and host families. We specialise in in-company language training for business, providing our clients with top-quality tailor-made courses such as programmes for banking and insurance. Due to an increasing demand from our corporate clients, we also developed translations and interpreting services as well as level testing services for promotion or recruitment.

In 2006, after having trained the staff of IDA Ireland for four consecutive years, Lernen Language School was recognised by the European Personnel Selection Office (EPSO) for testing their staff adequately in the German language. In 2010, we were awarded with a contract with the European Foundation for the Improvement of Living and Working Conditions to train their staff in the German languages, which we have been awarded with again in 2014.

Why German


With its rich culture and robust economy, Germany has something for everyone, from exciting employment and business opportunities through a vibrant arts and music scene to a good education system with affordable universities. Are you looking to take advantage of Germany’s strong economy and employment opportunities? Or maybe you have a trip planned to Germany? Our German Courses in Dublin give you the opportunity to study the German language, so you can be confident using it within real life social and business situations.

No matter what your previous experience or level of German Language, our German lessons in Dublin can accommodate you. Whether you are a complete novice, looking to develop a basic level of German language, or you are quite comfortable using the German language but want to further your knowledge of German, our German lessons in Dublin will benefit you. Whatever your level of language, Atlas Language School have a solution for you.

Lernen Language School run German courses in Dublin for people of varying ages and abilities. Because of close genetic links, German and English share many features, and have some very similar words. This can make the study of the German language slightly easier for those who natively speak English. Our German language courses in Dublin are run by highly qualified native speakers with extensive and varied experience of teaching environments in Ireland and the rest of the world.
