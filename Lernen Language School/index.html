Why learn German?

  • German has the largest number of native speakers in the EU
  • Germany is the worldst second largest exporter
  • German and English are similar. Many words in German sound and/or look the same as equivalent English words, because the two languages share the same “grandparent. For example, look at these words:”
    • Haus = house
    • Buch = book
    • Finger = finger
  • Knowing German creates business opportunities.
  • Gain access to German-speaking cultures, art, music, literature and film.
  • Germany is home to numerous international corporations
