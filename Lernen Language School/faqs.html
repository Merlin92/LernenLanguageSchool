Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


Registration and Payment

How do I register?
As soon as you have decided that you would like to participate in a course, you can either visit us in person or send us your registration form by post, by fax or online. Any registrations made over the telephone must also be confirmed in writing.

Will I receive confirmation of the registration?
We do not normally issue any registration confirmations. However, please inform us if you would like to receive a written confirmation. We will then confirm your registration in writing.

Which language level should I tick on the registration form?
Before you register you should complete a short grading test with us. This will allow us to work out which course would best suit your language proficiency. If you register in writing and do not know which language level corresponds to your proficiency, please let us know how long or how many hours you have spent learning German.

Do I need to pay the course fee when I register?
Yes, the course fee must be paid straight away. This is the only way to reserve a place on a course.

Will you inform me when I have paid the course fee?
We do not normally send out any confirmation when we receive the course fee. However, if you would like to receive written confirmation, please let us know when you register and we will inform you when we have received the money.

Do I need to register for the entire course straight away?
You can pay for a course which comprises several modules either in full or module-by-module. In the latter case, you only pay for one module at first, and then extend your registration if you wish to continue learning. Please note, however, that written confirmation of the registration will only be provided for the period for which you have paid.

How can I pay?
You can pay the course fee by bank transfer or in person/through a contact person at our office.

Lessons and Programme

How long is a single lesson?
A lesson lasts 45 minutes.

When do the lessons take place?
The lessons take place each day from 8:15 to 11:30 am or from 12 noon to 3:15 pm.
Those booking a course with 30 hours of lessons per week will have lessons from 8:15 am to 1:30 pm. Private lessons are arranged individually.
The Evening Class for Beginners takes place from Monday to Thursday, from 6 to 7:30 pm; whilst the Advanced Evening Class runs from 6 to 9:15 pm.

Where do the lessons take place?
The lessons take place at our premises see the Contact Us page for the address.

Can I complete an official examination?
Yes, we offer the following examinations:
German A1 to C1
German test for immigrants
TestDaF
German Certified Business Test

Are there holidays?
There are no holidays during the year. There are, however, no lessons on public holidays and between the 24th December and 1st January.

Can I take a break from a course if I want to go on holiday?
You you can, of course, go on holiday. However, we are unable to refund the course fees for the period that you miss due to holidays.

How do I find the right language course for me?
Before beginning the course, take part in a grading test. This will allow us to work out which course would best suit your language proficiency.

Are there computers at the Language Academy?
There are several computers with Internet access, which participants in our courses can use free of charge.

How many people are learning German at the Lernen Language School and which countries are they from?
Each month, over 300 people learn German at different levels at the Lernen Language School Most of these students are between 18 and 25 years old, but there are also lots of older participants. The minimum age is 16. People from a total of 30 countries are learning German with us.

Arrival

Where is The School?
Lernen is situated on in East of Ireland, in Co. Dublin, see the Contact Us page for more info.

How do you travel to Dublin??
If arriving by car, Dublin is situated on the East of the M1 motorway which connect to the M50. Dublin can also be easily reached by train, bus (City Link, Bus Eireann, Go Bus) and has an international airport (Dublin City Airport). You can fly to Dublin, and travel to the city by train or bus.

How do I reach my accommodation??
When you arrive at Dublin City Airport, you can take a bus to the city centre (O'Connell Street). You can also take a taxi from the airport direct to your accommodation. If you land at a different airport, travel to Dublin by train. Then take a taxi from Dublin train station.
We can also collect you directly from all airports by taxi and take you to your accommodation. Please let us know at least 3 days before your arrival and send us your exact flight details. If you would like to arrange a transfer for your return journey, please do so at our secretary's office.

How do I find the Lernen Language School once I am in Dublin??
The Lernen Language School is situated in the centre of town, 5 minutes from the main train station.

General Questions

Who can participate in the Language Courses for Young People program?
Our language courses for young people program is built for teenagers and young adults between 14 and 21 years of years who want to learn a foreign language course with their own peer group while taking a trip abroad. Each program destination specializes in specific age groups, which means that the students are always put into groups with their peers.

What's involved in the Language Courses for Young People program?
Our Courses for Young People program teaches students language skills in a relaxed learning atmosphere, without the boredom or stress of everyday school life. The initial uncertainty of being in a new place, learning a new language, goes away quickly and then learning grammar and vocabulary becomes fun - Mainly because the students don’t have to do it alone, rather they get to learn with old and new friends!

In the afternoons, we offer a variable recreational program, which gradually introduces the juniors to the country, its people and its culture.
Participants will travel supervised and supported during the language program according to their age.

What's included in the Language Courses for Young People package price?
Our Courses for Young People program price package includes:

Language course as booked
Learning materials
Accommodations and meal plans as booked
Round-the-clock supervision by a qualified team leader
Transportation by bus or plane to desired destination; if necessary, transfer to and from the airport
Organized excursions
Many more leisure time/ recreational activities
