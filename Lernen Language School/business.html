Business & Executive German Language Courses



Confidence in communicating through German is a vital tool for conducting successful business in Germany and our Business & Executive Courses are custom designed to deliver fast and effective training.

General Business German Course Information

The Business German course consists of 20 hours tuition per week of General German + 5 hours tuition per week of specific business related classes. The course is open to students with at least an upper intermediate level of German. Quality German Language teaching combined with small classes guarantees results.
The business section of the course includes the following business modules:
  • Participating in meetings & discussions
  • Negotiating successfully
  • Using the telephone effectively
  • Giving clear and concise presentations
  • Entertaining clients
  • Learning strategies for the future

Executive German Language Courses

Our tailored Executive Courses cater for the specific and general needs of our professional students. Courses are offered in boardroom facilities at our city centre college located right in the heart of Dublin's Commercial Centre. We cater for individuals and small groups who have a limited time to focus on their language needs. We have state of the art facilities and fully trained staff with a strong Business Teaching background who can identify and address our students' specific needs. Following a detailed needs analysis, a tailored 1:1 programme is developed for each student.

In - Company German Language Training

  • Germen Training for Call Centres
  • German Training for Human Resources
  • German Training for Sales & Marketing
  • German Training for Team Leaders
  • German Business Skills

International House has established itself as Ireland's leading in-house language training company working with companies such as Kellogg's, Google, Endesa Ireland, Accenture and Allianz. This has been achieved by working with its client companies to design specific German language courses that address the language needs of employees depending on the industry and type of business. The central focus of our courses is active communication. We aim to improve overall active communication skills: speaking (with a strong focus on pronunciation) both face to face and on the telephone, writing and listening (understanding different accents). Courses can be arranged on site at your workplace at a time to suit your employees or we can arrange classes at our city centre college on Dawson Street, Dublin 2. After a detailed language audit of your company’s language requirements, we will be happy to provide a tailor-made proposal and quotation together with training options.
